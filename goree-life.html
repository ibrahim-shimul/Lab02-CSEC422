Gorée Life

A mobile app designed to boost tourism on Gorée Island, Senegal, by offering easy access to essential services and a digital journal for travelers.

Overview

Gorée Island is a historic site with a rich cultural heritage. However, tourists often face challenges in finding reliable information about local attractions, accommodations, and dining options. Gorée Life addresses this by providing a comprehensive mobile guide that simplifies the travel experience.

The Problem

Visitors to Gorée Island lacked a centralized platform to book tickets, find guided tours, and locate amenities. This fragmentation led to missed opportunities for local businesses and a less-than-optimal experience for tourists.

The Solution

We designed an intuitive mobile application that serves as a personal travel companion. Key features include:

  • Ticket Booking: Seamless purchase of ferry and museum tickets.
  • Interactive Map: Easy navigation to landmarks, hotels, and restaurants.
  • Digital Journal: A space for users to document their memories with photos and notes.
  • Local Guides: Direct connection to certified tour guides.
