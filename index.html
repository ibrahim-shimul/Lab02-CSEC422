My Recent Works

Gorée Life

Gorée Life is a mobile app that boosts tourism on Gorée Island, Senegal. It offers easy access to tickets, guided tours, museum info, hotels, restaurants, and an interactive map. Users can also maintain a digital journal to capture their experiences.

Les Pinks

Les Pinks is a creative platform celebrating the artistry and style of Les Pinks. The project showcases their work through a clean, visually engaging digital experience, allowing users to explore, connect, and immerse themselves in the brand’s unique vision.
